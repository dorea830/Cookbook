Instant Ramen

Ingredients:

  • An instant ramen package of your choice
  • Boiled water
  • Chopped green onions

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Open vegetable and soup flavoring packets and dump into boiling water
  3. Place ramen gently into the boiling water
  4. Cook for 3-5 minutes and stir gently when noodles start to soften
  5. Turn off fire and transfer soup and noodles into a bowl
  6. Garnish with green onions on top
  7. Enjoy with side dishes or by itself!

Tea Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 3 black tea teabags
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 star anise
  • Water
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Put the eggs in a pot and cover with water. Bring to a boil, and then remove pot from heat. Let stand 10 minutes, then drain water and let eggs cool.
  2. Gently crack the eggs with the back of a spoon, but do not peel them.
  3. Put cracked eggs in a single layer in a pot. Add water, teabags, soy sauce, star anise, and sugar just to cover. Bring to a boil, and then simmer on low heat for an hour. Turn eggs over if water level gets low.
  4. Remove pot from heat and let it cool to room temperature.
  5. Refrigerate until ready to eat. Leave the eggs in the liquid. When ready to eat, peel the eggs and enjoy!

Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • Cooked meat of your choice
  • Soy sauce
  • Rice (at least a day-old preferred)
  • Chopped green onions

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a hot, oiled pan. Scramble eggs until halfway cooked, then transfer onto a plate.
  2. Put rice in pan and evenly spread out.
  3. Add meat to pan and stir-fry contents. After the rice and meat are warmed, add eggs back into pan and green onions.
  4. Add soy sauce and stir-fry until soy sauce is evenly distributed.
  5. When everything is cooked, transfer to a plate and enjoy!
